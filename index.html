About Us

Bullhead Leather is a veteran-owned business, founded in 2020 by a passionate craftsman in Chippewa Falls, WI. In a time where everything hand-made is dubbed "artisan," regardless of the quality; Bullhead Leather was established around the principle that our goods will be heirloom-quality. These are products that withstand the rigors of time and are meant to be carried everyday and eventually passed on as an heirloom to a worthy recipient.
